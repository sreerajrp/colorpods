Active Noise Cancellation

Control what you hear. And what you don’t.

Active Noise Cancellation

Active Noise Cancellation reduces unwanted sounds so you can immerse yourself in what you’re listening to or simply stay focused. And Transparency mode lets you comfortably hear the world around you.

Personalised Spatial Audio

Immersive sound. Fine‑tuned
to you.

Personalised Spatial Audio

Personalised Spatial Audio2 brings you 3D sound suited to your specific ear shape for a theatre-like experience that surrounds you with sound.

loud noise.

Hearing Health

AirPods Pro 2 includes Hearing Health features like Loud Sound Reduction, Conversation Boost and Background Sounds.

Siri

Your always-on intelligent assistant.

Siri

Control your audio, get directions, check the weather and more with a simple “Hey Siri”. With Announce Notifications, Siri can automatically read important notifications as they arrive. And with Siri Interactions, you can nod your head yes or shake your head no when Siri asks if you’d like to hear a message, answer a call or manage other notifications.

Magical Experience
